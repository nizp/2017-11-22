实现一个LazyMan，可以按照以下方式调用: LazyMan("Hulk")输出: Hi! This is Hank!   LazyMan("Hulk").sleep(10).eat("dinner")输出 Hi! This is Hulk! //等待1秒.. Wake up after 1000ms Eat dinner~   LazyMan("Hulk").eat("dinner").eat("supper")输出 Hi This is Hulk! Eat dinner~ Eat supper~   LazyMan("Hulk").sleepFirst(5).eat("supper")输出 //等待5秒.. Wake up after 5000ms Hi This is Hulk! Eat supper   以此类推。
